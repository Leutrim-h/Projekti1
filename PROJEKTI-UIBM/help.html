Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu and click the "Buy" button next to the product you want.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards and cash on delivery.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 10 minutes after placing it by calling our support team.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location.
